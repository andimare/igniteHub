Graphical Design

You’ll explore the trends impacting the design world: interactive design, “thumb friendly” typography, Design Thinking and more. You’ll also learn transferable skills, or soft skills, that will help boost your professional life, wherever you take it.

Programming

This course will provide an introduction to programming languages, starting from basic concepts and progressing to more advanced topics. Students will learn the syntax and structure of the language of choice, as well as how to create and use functions, manipulate data structures, work with files, Frameworks, Libraries. Additionally, each module will include a project where students will apply the concepts covered in the module to a real-world problem.

Wed Design

Web design is a fascinating field that combines creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills. It’s about creating digital spaces that are visually appealing, user-friendly, and accessible to all. Studying web design can open doors to various career paths. You could work as a freelance designer, join a tech company, or even start your own web design agency. Plus, with the digital world constantly evolving, there’s always something new to learn and explore.
